About Us

TripeF Global Ltd. is committed to making safe, sustainable water solutions accessible to every home, school, and business in Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet urgent: to make clean, safe water accessible to every Nigerian while tackling the environmental crisis caused by plastic pollution. By reducing reliance on packaged water, we help communities hydrate better—safely, affordably, and sustainably.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every Nigerian practices sustainable hydration—a lifestyle where pure water is always within reach, free from contaminants, and delivered through eco-friendly systems that support both human and environmental health.
